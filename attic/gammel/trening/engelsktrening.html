Welcome to Svømmegruppa NTNUI.

As an NTNUI member you can go swimming for free on the following days:



 
 
 

Day Time
Monday 21.30-22.30
Friday 20.00-21.30
Sunday 19.00-20.30

The swimming is only for NTNUI members, so remember to bring along you NTNUI membershipcard and 10kr for your locker.
 

The swimming is in "Sentralbadet". To find it, just follow Elgesetergata into Prinsensgate and walk for another 50 meters and you should be right there. It is a large building on your right hand side opposite the nightclub "Monte Cristo".
